Everyday cooking, for everyday people

All those sayings about cooking being an artform makes the whole concept terrifying. What on earth does it implicate, and how is the Average Joe supposed to create artwork on a day-to-day basis? I have busted this myth – fancy cooking is not so fancy that it is unattainable. It should an activity that is pleasurable, exciting, allowing your imagination to run free, and so easy that even dad can do it.

Be inspired by my everyday recipes and impress your family easily made refined meals or bring your own cooking ideas to the table through the feedback form!
